WELCOME TO HAFFEZ QURAN

ABOUT US

Hafez is an integrated electronic platform for distance learning teaching and memorization of the Noble Quran, as we connect those who wish to memorize the Quran everywhere with a distinguished elite of specialized teachers in an easy interactive way and a flexible educational schedule that corresponds to the fast-paced lifestyle.

Hafez’s team aims to facilitate learning and memorization of the Quran for all Muslims by the Internet in the correct and optimal way.

Read More

Our Mission

Creating an interactive electronic platform that enables Muslims everywhere to memorize and contemplate the Quran and reduce the difficulties and obstacles they face through an easy and flexible system that is compatible with their lifestyle and facilitates the return and kindness of the Book of Allah.

Our Vision

Hafez becomes the largest online platform that enables anyone around the world to memorize and master the Noble Quran easily and Proficiency From a distance.

Our Message

Facilitating the contemplate and memorization of the Noble Quran for all Muslims women, Men and children - by certified teachers in an easy and interactive way over the Internet. The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said: "The best of you is who learns the Quran and offer to teaches it."

Why Haffez

Hafez offers all the lessons online by an interactive face-to-face experience with an elite group of instructors of the Quran.

With you wherever you are

All lessons are taught by distance learning through audio and video episodes, so you can continue your Memorization from any place without any restrictions or difficulty and through a flexible schedule.

Save your time

You do not need to spend a lot of time to reach a specific place, just make sure you have a good network and that your device is available. For all family members, we have a distinguished group of sheikhs who specialize in adult and children's memorization. with us in Hafez, you can follow your children's lessons and your lessons from one place.

Precision and perfection

Hafiz provides you with a selection of certified teachers to ensure the quality and perfection of recitation and intonation.

Integrated programs

We provide you with integrated programs and periodic evaluation to follow a specific approach to memorizing and learning the Quran with the provisions of intonation and recitation from the beginning until the completion of memorizing the Noble Quran.

Specialized Female teachers

Hafez facilitates the way for every Muslim woman to memorize the Noble Quran by specialized female teachers.

24/7 hours support

Hafez team provides you with full support to facilitate and overcome the obstacles and difficulties that stand in the way of mastering the Quran through: Chat system.

Our Honorable Sheikhs

We have chosen for you a group of specialized and certified sheikhs in memorizing the Noble Quran to ensure that the Book of Allah is memorized and that its rulings are learned and recited in the correct way. To enable everyone to memorize and recite the Book of Allah, Hafiz Provides specialized female teachers for the memorization of girls and women.

We have also made sure to provide a distinguished group specialized in dealing with different age groups, including children and adults, and various abilities to meet all needs with the latest educational methods.
